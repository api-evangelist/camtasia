Transitions are a pacing device that can guide viewer attention, smooth disjointed clips, and make videos more engaging. These post-production special effects explain the video’s main points and enhance viewer emotion by providing an intuitive storytelling cue. Camtasia’s video transitions prioritize flow, not flash.
