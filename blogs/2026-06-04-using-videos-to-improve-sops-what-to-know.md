Standard operating procedures are the backbone of any team aiming for consistency, efficiency, and compliance, yet traditional text-based documentation often fails to engage employees effectively. Video SOPs offer a more dynamic approach to training and procedure documentation through visuals, audio, and motion. The article recommends Camtasia Editor and Camtasia Snagit for creating and managing video SOPs.
