New employees often feel overwhelmed during their first days at work, and video onboarding programs provide accessible, on-demand guidance to help new hires understand company culture, tools, and processes. These structured video collections deliver consistent training at scale while reducing pressure on both trainers and employees. Camtasia Editor, Camtasia Snagit, and Camtasia Audiate are recommended for building onboarding content.
