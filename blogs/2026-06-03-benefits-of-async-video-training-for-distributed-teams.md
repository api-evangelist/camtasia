Asynchronous video training represents a practical communication method for remote and distributed teams. Async training keeps learners aligned without disrupting their work schedules and offers advantages over live training sessions. Key benefits include flexibility across time zones, consistent messaging, self-paced learning, and the ability to reuse and update content over time using Camtasia and TechSmith tools.
